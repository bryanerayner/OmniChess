Welcome to Chess! In a few weeks you might be able to even play this classic game in the browser! Until then, just look at this cross browser implementation of the details element. Enjoy!

Piece Movement

Pawn

Pawns can be moved forward one square at a time, and capture only on the upwards diagonal.

Bishop

Bishops can be moved on diagonal lines.

Knight

Knights can be moved in an "L" shape - Two in one direction, and one in a perpendicular direction.

Rook

Rooks can be moved horizontally & vertically.

Queen

Queens can be moved on diagonal lines as well as horizontal & vertical.

King

Kings can be moved in all directions, but only one space at a time.
